About Me




As a recent grad from the University of Waterloo, I grew a passion for creating meaningful content through storytelling that has snowballed into the search for a career in copywriting. My studies in communications, semiotics and rhetoric led me down enlightening roads of exposing people to brands across very different realms.

From creating a Movember event, to evolving a social startup, to selling memberships in a luxury club my work has taught me a lot. You'll find some of the pieces most important to me on this page.
